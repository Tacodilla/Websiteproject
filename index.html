Our Story

Crafted by hand, worn from the heart

I’m Emma — a self‑taught jewelry maker who fell in love with tiny tools and the quiet alchemy of metal and stone. Every piece starts as a sketch in my notebook, then is shaped, soldered, polished, and finished in my sunny home studio.

I favor responsibly sourced metals, lab‑grown stones where possible, and packaging that’s gentle on the planet. My designs blend vintage romance and modern minimalism: delicate chains, personalized initials, and just‑right sparkle you can wear every day.

Whether you’re celebrating a milestone or treating yourself “just because,” I hope you find something that feels like you. Curious to learn? Join my small, friendly crafting classes — beginners welcome!
